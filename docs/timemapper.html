TIME MAPPER

This page is meant to hold my Time Mapper visual learning aid created on the Time Mapper website. I decided to document the brief history of coffee, as I think there are some interesting parallels between the bean-based drink's popularity and other social movements that happened around the same time.

The common thread I was trying to weave with this timeline is the parallel evolution of coffee and its popularity with the development of intellectual movements based around the challenging of the status quo. For centuries, beer, a 'downer' substance, was the drink of choice for England, all throughout the Middle Ages. Then, just as coffee starts spreading around the world, so too do new ideas begin to take shape, both in England with the Royal Society being formed out of a coffeehouse club, and with the Revolution sprouting in the American colonies.
